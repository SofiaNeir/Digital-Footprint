A digital footprint (sometimes called a digital shadow or electronic footprint) refers to the trail of data you leave behind when you use the Internet. This includes the websites you visit and the emails and information you send online. A fingerprint can be used to track a person's online activities and devices

Tips for taking care of your Digital Footprint

Do not use the same password for more than one account Use difficult passwords Divide your accounts into two groups, one with important information and others not so relevant. Close accounts you no longer use Do not save your passwords on shared computers or systems
